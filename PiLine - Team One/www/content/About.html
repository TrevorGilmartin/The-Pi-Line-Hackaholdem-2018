Welcome to the Depths Pi-Liners

On the 3rd of February 2018 a momentous event occurrent known only by the name Hackaholdem. Here three budding adventures were tasked with the privilege of coming up with an application based around the theme of connecting people. After much deliberation the three came up with the idea of making an offline file sharing and communication network that runs off inexpensive off-the-shelf hardware which they coined The Pi-Line

After a presentation in front of a group if their peers, colleagues and frenemies in which The Pi-Line got some keen interest the group went hard at development. That was until the unfortunate loss of one of the adventures thus lead to two remaining those left went by the Names of,

Trevor Gilmartin
       &
Robert Cormican

These illustrious few continued to work on the proposed idea. Robert on the hardware aspects of a raspberry pi and Trevor on the html and CSS that would make the site page. Their tale is legendary and can be heard throughout the hollow hall of the fine Building Block Establishment
